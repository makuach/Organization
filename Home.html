BACKGROUND

United Community Support Organization (UCSO)
is a South Sudanese humanitarian organization that
was established in 2016. The establishment of UCSO
was necessitated by the departure of international
NGOs due to worsening insecurity in many disaster
areas in the country. As a result, an acute lack
of the most essential services in these areas turned
instantly worse. Therefore, UCSO started
operating in these disaster areas in order
to help turn the situation around together with partner organizations.
