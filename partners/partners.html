THE REPUBLIC OF
SOUTH SUDAN
MINISTRY OF HEALTH
(MOH)

The Ministry of Health of the Republic of South Sudan
mission is to improve the health status of the people
by effective delivery of Basic Package of Health
and Nutrition Services (BPHNS) through:

  • provision of health promotion;
  • disease, injury, and disability prevention;
  • treatment and rehabilitation services, with
    full participation of the people.
