Our Vision

To be recognized as an organization that delivers quality
programs, services, and models of community
development that positively impact the social, health,
and economic lives of children, families, and
communities.

Mandate

  1. Maintain compliance with all applicable laws with
    the authorities at the National, State, and County
    levels.
  2. Create and retain a database of information and
    research on topics of interest to our target
    beneficiaries.
  3. Create and maintain a strong presence in
    disaster areas through all seasons to offer support to
    families, communities, and organizations

Our Mission

To promote sustainable community development by
implementing health, educational, preventative domestic
violence, economic and technical assistance programs.

Core Values

To fulfill its mandate and realize its vision and mission,
UCSO will be guided by its core values of:

  • commitment

  • Accountability

  • Sacrifice

  • Integrity

  • Continous Quality Improvement

  • Risk and Innovation

  • Cooperation
